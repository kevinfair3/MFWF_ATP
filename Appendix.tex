\section{DOE Design Matrix}
\label{app:doe_matrix}

This appendix documents the full DOE design matrix for both Series~R (curved trailing edge) and Series~Z (trapezoidal planform). All vein angles are measured from the conventional negative $y$-axis pointing toward the trailing edge at the root.Pattern~U distributes the three veins with uniform angular spacing across the chord; Pattern~V concentrates vein spacing progressively toward the leading edge; Pattern~W concentrates vein spacing progressively toward the trailing edge. Each design was manufactured in 2 replicates and planned for 3 frequency test conditions per replicate. All frequency test entries are pending due to ATP mechanism unavailability this year.
